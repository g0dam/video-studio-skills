## Reusable Prompt Blocks

### Visual Lock

Original family-friendly animated musical adventure, storybook 3D animation look without referencing any existing studio or IP, expressive appealing character with a clear silhouette, warm magical realism, tactile handmade materials, color-keyed lighting that guides the eye, clear theatrical staging, no readable text, no logos, no copied characters.

### Motion Lock

Start with a still emotional question, then a gentle push-in as the hero notices the magical object. Use anticipation before the hand reaches forward, soft follow-through on hair and fabric, environmental motion responding to rhythm, a short breath pause, then a warm reveal with floating particles and a community or family reaction beat.

### Continuity Lock

Keep the hero's face, hair, wardrobe, charm, and emotional posture consistent across scenes. Keep the magic tied to one original symbol and one material language. Maintain location geography: outsider edge, threshold or doorway, central gathering space, and final wonder reveal. Put precise captions, lyrics, and dialogue in post-production.
