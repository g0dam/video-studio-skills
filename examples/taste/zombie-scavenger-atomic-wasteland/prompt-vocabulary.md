## Reusable Prompt Blocks

### Visual Lock

An ultra-wide atom-punk seaside wasteland: broken luxury resort glass, cyan ocean glare, sun-bleached orange light, cracked shopping streets, rusted metal and faded teal signage. A non-human service-worker hero with black leather silhouette and simple LED face emotion stands centered against the dead world, with stylized zombie pressure in the background and one lonely companion object introduced as a ritual focus.

### Motion Lock

Start with a slow reveal from a damaged resort detail to the ocean horizon, then track the worker-hero through one clean cleanup action. Background zombies advance slowly while dust, surf, and debris move across frame. Hold for one LED-face reaction, then reverse the scene's meaning with a small tender gesture toward a mannequin or found object.

### Continuity Lock

Keep the hero's body material, LED face color language, hat/coat silhouette, main tool or weapon, mount/vehicle logic, and seaside geography consistent across all shots. Repeat no more than six world icons per sequence: worker, dead resort, dead street, mount/vehicle, zombie mass, lonely companion object. Keep red graphic typography as punctuation only, not exposition.
