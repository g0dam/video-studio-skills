# Prompt Vocabulary: 爽剧动漫效果

## Positive Vocabulary

- camera: vertical short-video framing, aggressive push-in, impact zoom, low-angle dominance shot, eye close-up, object insert, reaction close-up, over-the-shoulder confrontation
- motion: sudden pause before reversal, sharp head turn, aura flare, speed-line burst, cape or coat snap, phone vibration reveal, token glint, dust shockwave
- color: cel-shaded high contrast, hard rim light, electric blue aura, gold status glow, red pressure accents, white flash impact, crisp black linework
- texture: manga panel split, speed lines, floating system UI, cracked floor, glass reflection, metal token, contract paper, screen glow, energy particles
- sound: breath pause, low-frequency rumble, bass drop, whoosh, impact hit, glass crack, crowd gasp, short decisive line
- editing: freeze frame, black-frame hit, white flash, reaction triplet, match cut from object to face, fast setup and delayed payoff beat
- performance: restrained protagonist, smug antagonist, shocked crowd, chin-lift dominance gesture, cold eye glint, small smile after reveal

## Negative Vocabulary

- avoid: exact anime franchise style, copied character designs, living creator imitation, dense legal text, tiny unreadable phone UI, long continuous fight choreography, crowded face-heavy scenes, excessive subtitles
- common AI failures: malformed hands during action, unstable faces in crowds, unreadable Chinese text, flickering system panels, overbusy particle effects, unclear subject hierarchy, accidental parody tone

## Reusable Prompt Blocks

### Visual Lock

Original anime-inspired high-payoff short drama look, 9:16 vertical composition, cel-shaded characters with crisp linework, readable conflict in the first frame, protagonist under pressure in foreground or center, antagonist imposing from a higher angle, hard rim light, red-blue tension palette shifting to gold/electric-blue power glow at the reversal, speed lines and manga-panel accents only on impact beats.

### Motion Lock

Start with a tense static hold, then a slow push toward the protagonist's eyes. Pause for half a beat before the reveal. Trigger a sharp impact zoom on the key object or eye glint, add a one-frame white flash, cut to shocked reaction close-ups, then settle into a low-angle dominance pose with aura particles moving slowly behind the protagonist.

### Continuity Lock

Keep the protagonist calm and visually consistent: same face, hairstyle, outfit, key token, and eye color across shots. Keep the antagonist's power posture clear before the reversal and broken posture after it. Maintain a simple location geography: confrontation zone, key object position, crowd line, and exit path. Put all accurate Chinese captions, UI labels, legal text, and phone messages in post-production when precision is required.
